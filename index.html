Career takeoff

By uniting retail, convenience and food & beverage under one brand, we are reimagining travel, transcending traditional boundaries to create a seamless, end-to-end experience with you, the traveler, at its heart. More diversified and resilient, Avolta exists to make travelers happier.

Our diverse workforce, representing over 150 nationalities, is the drum beat behind our vision to become the most innovative and successful travel experience player. Each team member strives to bring Avolta to life every day, turning our collective aspirations into achievements.

Our recruitment process

1

Submit Your Application

Start your journey by applying through our career portal; upload your CV and share a bit about yourself.

2

Initial Screening

We will review your application to assess the alignment between your skills and the job requirements.

3

Interview Process

Engage with us through one or more interviews to discuss your experiences, skills, and aspirations.

4

Assessment

Depending on the role, you may be invited to complete assignments or assessments to showcase your capabilities.

5

Offer & Onboarding

If it's a match, we'll extend an offer. Once accepted, we'll guide you through every step of your onboarding journey.
